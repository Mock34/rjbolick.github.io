ACT Score Report

Role
UX Designer, User Researcher
Company
Mindspire Test Prep
Year
2019
Type
Data Analytics
Background

Mindspire Test Prep offers premier tutoring services for nearly any subject or test, including the ACT. Each student takes a practice test at the start of tutoring to establish a baseline, which informs the tutor’s plan of work and helps the student visualize goals. Mindspire’s existing ACT practice test report was not being utilized because of poor UI and UX, leading to difficult onboarding of new students and even loss of market share.

Solution

I overhauled Mindspire’s practice test reporting system from Excel reports to Tableau visualizations, which required restructuring and coding all data collection methods. Information that spanned over 5 pages was thoughtfully condensed into a single screen dashboard. New features like test prediction, area of opportunity, and percentile statistics were also added.

Outcomes

This report is currently being used at Mindspire and has drastically reduced the time needed to generate one. After teaching several members of the east coast team at Tableau how to make and use the visualization, it now serves as a training aid at the company.
