About me

I'm an engineer and designer with a passion for research driven innovation and user-centric implementation.

Currently, I'm part of the team at Infinity AI where I build synthentic data generator APIs for ML models and web tools such as the Infinty AI Marketplace.

I've created design systems, designed FDA cleared surgical instruments, apps, enterprise software, APIs, and more. This breadth of experience has taught me to ask questions. Questions - not answers - have led to some of the best designs I've been a part of.

Trail running, cycling, and crazy adventures keep my mind and body sharp when not designing (although sometimes these intersect with my designs).
