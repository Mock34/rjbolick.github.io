Industry
Technnology
Background

Ridesharing services have made getting around quick and easy, but this convenience isn’t without risks. Inadequate safety measures have led to dangerous and deadly situations, and accessibility concerns have been largely ignored. These problems compound with inadequate UX, leaving many users confused or with a bad experience. It’s time to rethink ridesharing.

Solution

Shyft was designed with accessibility and safety at the forefront. Gone are the fancy and ineffective visuals of other apps; with Shyft, anybody can find the features they need. Everything from onboarding to arrival was designed from the ground up and tested with users. A focused UI with rich features delivers a delightful and functional user experience.

Outcomes

This project was delivered to faculty and students at North Carolina State University and members of the Experienced Innovators of the Triangle. It received positive feedback and all were able to use the app and navigate its features.
