Nutrics
AI Powered Meal Suggestions

Nutrics helps you discover new food by adapting to your tastes with artificial intelligence. It suggests meals you'll love - not menus.
I built this app with data scientists and computer engineers at the Big Ideas Lab and behavioral psychologists at the Center for Advanced Hindsight. I created the visual design, built live mockups in Adobe XD for user testing, and contributed to the feature engineering process which informs the predictive models.
